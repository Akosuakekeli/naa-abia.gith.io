About

Experience new cultures and the life-changing benefits of travel. Expand your horizons. Meet new people. Learn how to be more empathetic. Be exposed to innovative investment opportunities. We are Chasing Chass Travel Our mission is to create a culture of travel that prioritizes visiting underexposed, underserved, and diverse locations across the globe. We are inspired by the journey and travel experiences of our founder, Chassidy Keys. We believe that nothing can grow in one's comfort zone and we encourage people to cultivate their character and understanding of others by visiting unique, impactful places around the world. Travel and tourism make the world go round, and give you the opportunity to become a better global citizen! We recently launched the "Rethink Africa" tour to expose people to the wonders and workings of Africa. Our next tour group will experience Accra, Ghana in December 2021, where attendees will immerse themselves in the history and culture of that land. In addition, the tour will provide opportunity to participate in forward-thinking investment opportunities that can spur equity and growth. In line with our mission to create a culture of travel which promotes meaningful diversity and inclusion, we are sponsoring two youths of the African diaspora who reside in an underserved communities in the US and Caribbean and will extend them the opportunity to gain work experience in an Assistant Manager, Global Experiences apprenticeship. Join us and be a part of something that will change your life.
